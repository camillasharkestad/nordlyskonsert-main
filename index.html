ÁLFR — a Northern Lights Concert

ÁLFR brings together music and spectacular visuals inspired by the aurora borealis, wild landscapes and the fairytale feeling of the North. The concert is designed as an atmospheric journey between the warm human voice and the cold, shimmering sky.
