ÁLFR — a Northern Lights Concert

Welcome to a magical concert performance at Forum Scene.

Majestic music, spectacular nature and dancing northern lights.

ÁLFR — a Northern Lights Concert is a scenic concert experience where Eilif Stene’s images of Norwegian nature and the aurora borealis form the visual thread throughout the performance.

The music and lyrics are composed by Jørn Lavoll and Marianne Juvik Sæbø, inspired by nature and the northern lights.

The atmospheric music moves in the borderland between game music and film music. On this occasion, it will be performed by the Royal Norwegian Navy Band, pianist and choir, conducted by Bjørn Sagstad. The music has been rearranged for concert band by Reid Gilje.

The vocalist is Marianne Juvik Sæbø.

About Marianne

Marianne Juvik Sæbø

Marianne Juvik Sæbø is an experienced and versatile singer based in Bergen/Askøy, known for her warm voice, musical presence and ability to create moments that feel personal, beautiful and memorable.

With classical training from Bergen and London, she brings both quality and sensitivity to every performance. Her repertoire ranges from hymns, folk music and gospel to classical works, musical theatre and opera.

Marianne can be booked for concerts, church ceremonies, funerals, memorial services and private events. She works closely with each client to choose music that suits the occasion — whether the atmosphere should be solemn, comforting, joyful or grand.

Her current spotlight is ÁLFR: a Nordic concert experience where music, storytelling and visual landscapes meet in an atmospheric universe inspired by nature and the northern lights.
